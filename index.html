Professor

Ibis Fonte

Ibis Fonte

is an Professsor at TechLaunch.io. Her career building software and embedded integrated technology spans for 25 years. She is the founder of the eCommerce and IoT company, the Software Factory.

She collaborated with the Miami Fashion Institute to bring FashionTech to the stage at Art Basel. She is well known as a local tech community leader, inspiring and mentoring others to pursue tech innovation and entrepreneurship. She is the STEM Evangelist for the scholarship foundation, BrinkerEducationInitiative.org. She is also a strong advocate for women in tech, a neuroscience and brain-computer interface enthusiast, and public speaker.


Students

WEB2000: Students will learn about interactivity for the web. This includes email marketing best practices using HTML, how to be more proficient with the bootstrap.js library, html5 semantic elements, basics of the shadow DOM, work with the canvas object, performance optimization of webpages, responsive web design, and the basics of UX design.
